Delete Your Account

You can delete your Cheap Cheap account at any time. Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

⚠️ This action is permanent. All your data will be deleted and cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile and login credentials
  • All grocery lists and saved items
  • Your savings history and streak data
  • Your app preferences and settings
  • Your push notification tokens

How to delete your account

Option 1 — In the app:

  1. Open Cheap Cheap
  2. Tap Account (bottom right)
  3. Scroll down and tap Settings
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion

Option 2 — Email request:

Send us an email from the address linked to your account and we will delete it within 30 days.
